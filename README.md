     # Insurance Analytics Dashboard

**Comprehensive Power BI Dashboard for Insurance Claims & Policy Analysis**

A modern, interactive Business Intelligence solution designed to track, analyze, 
and visualize insurance claims, policy performance, and customer metrics in real-time.


 What is Motive & Purpose
    ## 📊 Purpose & Motive

This dashboard was built to provide **actionable insights** into insurance operations by:

- **Track Claims Performance**: Monitor claim submission rates, approval rates, and processing times
- **Policy Analysis**: Analyze policy distribution, premium revenue, and customer retention metrics
- **Risk Assessment**: Identify high-risk claims and customer segments for targeted intervention
- **Operational Efficiency**: Measure KPIs like claim resolution time and cost-per-claim
- **Customer Insights**: Segment customers by demographics, policy type, and claim history
- **Executive Reporting**: Enable data-driven decision making through interactive visualizations

**Key Stakeholders**: Insurance managers, claims analysts, executives, and customer service teams

   Data Source
  ## 📁 Data Source

### Data Overview
- **Dataset**: Insurance Claims & Policy Database
- **Records**: ~10,000+ transactions
- **Time Period**: 2022-2024
- **File Format**: Excel (.xlsx) / CSV / SQL Database

### Data Tables Included

| Table | Description | Records |
|-------|-------------|---------|
| **Policies** | Policy details, customer info, plan type, premium | 5,000+ |
| **Claims** | Claim ID, status, amount, date filed, date processed | 8,000+ |
| **Customers** | Demographics, location, contact info, policy count | 4,500+ |
| **Claim Status** | Claim lifecycle (Submitted, Approved, Rejected, Paid) | 8,000+ |
| **Payments** | Premium payments, claim payouts, transaction dates | 12,000+ |
| **Customer Feedback** | Ratings, reviews, satisfaction scores, feedback | 3,000+ |

### Data Fields
- Policy Number, Customer ID, Policy Type (Auto, Health, Home)
- Claim Status (Pending, Approved, Rejected, Paid)
- Premium Amount, Claim Amount, Processing Time
- Customer Rating (1-5 stars), Feedback Comments
- Insurance Activity Dates (Policy Start, Claim Date, Payout Date)
- Customer Demographics (Age, Location, Policy Tenure)

### Data Privacy
✅ All personal identifiable information (PII) has been anonymized
✅ Sample/dummy data used for demonstration purposes
